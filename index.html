Research & Development

Myanmar Atlantic Co., Ltd, ( MAC ) studied with Marine Science Department, University of Myeik University (MU), for research and development programme. MAC and MU accomplished Six projects for R&D in 2013/14 and 2016/17 for studying environmental, marine species, biofouling organisms, phytoplankton culture, iversity of marine benthic macroalgae and food & feeding habit of oyster and Five projects in 2018/19 for Studying marine sediments of cultured pearling industry, Development of Myanmar culture pearl industries and myanmar pearl laws, Geographical analysis of land cover changes in the area of our pearl farm, Code of environmental practice private governance initiative and Primary knowledge of pearl & oyster.
